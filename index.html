FAQ

How large is it?
Our current prototype is the size of an iPhone. The finished version will be about 2" x 1" x .5"
Will the laser hurt my eyes?
No, it is low powered. However, do not point it directly at your eye for an extended period of time
Is this ethical?
Yes, PETA recommends excercising your pets with laser pointers
